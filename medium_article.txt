In our first two articles, we discussed LSTM and GRU models. In this article, we will include the widely used XGBoost model.

Method 3: XGBoost

XGBoost is a widely recognized model that can be utilized for stock prediction. It is often outperforming other algorithms on a variety of tasks.

Notably, its parallel processing capabilities significantly accelerate training times.

XGBoost can automatically handle missing values, thereby simplifying the data preprocessing process.

Most importantly, it offers comprehensive hyperparameter tuning options that provide detailed control over the performance of the model.

It is worth noting that XGBoost can be complicated to tune and understand, especially for beginners, due to the large number of hyperparameters.

Let’s get started then:
